Baseline Modeling Summary

Setup:
Target: occupancy_rate
Time-aware split: final 20 percent of unique dates used as test set
Baseline features: hotel identity, calendar seasonality, and occupancy lags 7, 14, 28 days
Trends model: baseline features plus top lagged Google Trends features from the EDA pass

Interpretation:
Best overall test RMSE was 4.7006 from baseline_plus_trends with RandomForest.
Best baseline-only RMSE was 5.8713.
The trends-augmented model improved RMSE by about 1.17 points.
This suggests lagged Google Trends adds useful signal beyond hotel seasonality and recent occupancy.
